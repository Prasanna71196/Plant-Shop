FAQ

What are the best indoor plants for low light? ▶
Snake plant, ZZ plant, and pothos are great for low-light conditions.
How often should I water my plants? ▶
It depends on the plant, but most need watering once a week or when soil is dry.
Are indoor plants safe for pets? ▶
Not all! Spider plant and areca palm are pet-safe, but lilies and pothos are toxic.
How do I increase humidity for my plants? ▶
Use a humidifier, mist plants, or place a water tray nearby.
Do I need fertilizer for indoor plants? ▶
Yes, use a balanced liquid fertilizer every 4–6 weeks during growing season.
